Artificial Intelligence and Data Science



What is AIDS (Artificial Intelligence and Data Science)?


AIDS is a specialized engineering program that blends Artificial Intelligence (AI) with Data Science (DS). It equips students to build intelligent systems and analyze large datasets for decision-making, prediction, and automation. The course focuses on both the development of AI models and extracting insights from data, making it highly relevant in today's data-driven world.

Core Areas Covered in AIDS:


  • Artificial Intelligence – Machine learning, deep learning, NLP

  • Data Science – Data preprocessing, analysis, and visualization

  • Mathematics – Probability, statistics, linear algebra

  • Programming – Python, R, Java, SQL

  • Big Data Technologies – Hadoop, Spark

  • Cloud Computing – AWS, Azure, Google Cloud

  • Data Engineering – ETL pipelines, data warehousing

  • Ethics and Privacy – Responsible AI, data governance

      • Skills You Gain:


      • Programming and problem-solving

      • AI model development and deployment

      • Data analysis and visualization

      • Working with large-scale datasets

      • Business intelligence and decision support

      • Cloud-based analytics and model hosting
